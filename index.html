Camera & Scanner Help

If your phone's camera doesn't open, try these steps:

  • Grant camera permission when the browser prompts.
  • Use the browser's site settings to allow camera if previously blocked.
  • Prefer a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox) and set camera to rear ('environment').
